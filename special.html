As for the previous editions, the VarI-COSI organizers will invite the authors of the presented papers and strong posters to submit their work for the next special issue publication.

The VarI-COSI 2018 Special Issue has been published on BMC Genomics and Medical Genomics and Bioinformatics. The accepted papers of the special issue are available at the BMC Genomics, BMC Medical Genomics and BMC Bioinformatics web pages.
