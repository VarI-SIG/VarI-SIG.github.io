Publication

In the previous editions of the VarI-COSI the organizers invited the authors of the presented papers and strong posters to submit their work for the next special issue publication. The selected manuscripts have been published in the following BioMed Central journals.

  • VarI-COSI 2016 special issue published on BMC Genomics, BMC Medical Genomics and BMC Bioinformatics.
  • VarI-COSI 2018 special issue published on BMC Genomics and BMC Biophysics.
  • VarI-COSI 2015 special issue published on BMC Genomics
  • VarI-COSI 2014 special issue published on BMC Genomics.
  • VarI-COSI 2013 special issue published on BMC Genomics
  • VarI-COSI 2012 special issue published on BMC Genomics
  • VarI-COSI 2011 special issue published on BMC Genomics
